Capital One Virtual Card

This project was a part of my internship, so, visually, there is not much I can display. The gist of the project was to unify the styling of 3 different webpages on the Capital One banking website. The 3 pages were the virtual card interface for 3 different merchants that provide the same functionality. Despite the similarity in functionality, the 3 webpages had many inconsisencies and different design choices due to them being create by different teams.

The first part of the project was to recreate the 3 pages from scratch to have a better understanding of the components and how they were initially built. I worked on a team of 6 interns and we split into pairs to each do a webpage.

That took about 2 weeks as we learned how to work together and use github as a team. The actual work was not very difficult as we were mainly working with css.

After that, we reconvened and decided on whose css styling to adopt/how to combine our 3 webpages. Then we worked together to standarized across our webpages. The work was a lot more git involved due to all 6 of us working at the same time. This really taught me how to collaborate on git and manage different conflict issues.

The final part of the internship was creating our presentation. This was pretty straightforward, but its the first time I presented to people that had higher stakes in the project/real professionals, so we spent a good about of time practicing and pitching our project.
